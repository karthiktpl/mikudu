How it works

Download

  • The only way to access mikudu blood donor network is to download the app from Google Play Store ( We will introduce iOS version later). Search for mikudu in Play Store and download it. It is free!

Registration

You need to register to be donor or to search for donors. Please create an account using your email id and complete the user profile. You can see this section right under the icon. You can choose how you want to be contacted â€“ there are three options, email, text message and call. Please uncheck the option(s) you do not want to be contacted on.

Search for blood banks

Tap on in your home screen and search for blood banks in your district by choosing the state and district where you need blood.

You can see contact details of most blood banks- please contact them and enquire if they can meet your requirements. Most of them will need you to arrange a blood donor in place of blood they give you.

Please tap on to find a blood donor.

We have the location information of some of the blood banks- click on to know the location. Please confirm the location with the blood bank before you visit them.

And here are some questions and answers to them we keep getting. Send us your questions on help@mikudu.com and we will add frequent ones here.

Who can donate blood?

  • Any healthy male or female between 18-65 years with a minimum weight of 45Kg and Hemoglobin level of 12.5 g/dL can donate blood. The donor should not have history of any severe illness or infection, Diabetes, hypertension, intake of medication or recent immunization. Those who have undergone any surgical procedure, needle usage, blood transfusion, Rabies treatment etc will have to wait for a year. Women cannot donate blood during Pregnancy, lactation or in menstrual cycles.

Do I have to pay donor or to contact donor?

  • No. This is a free app and taking money or other incentive to donate blood is illegal. Your blood bank/ hospital may charge you for processing blood as decided by authorities.
